# AI Module

> NOT LEGAL ADVICE — informational/educational; for attorney review (see SKILL.md
> disclaimer).

The AI-specific language for a PIIA. Use it whenever the user requests it, or suggest it when the
user describes a company with a workforce that regularly uses generative AI tools to create work
product.

> **Scope assumption.** This module assumes the company *builds on top of* third-party
> foundation models or uses other companies' AI-powered products — it does **not**
> train, fine-tune, or develop its own foundation models. Language about Company-owned
> models, model weights, and training data has therefore been omitted. If the company
> does train or fine-tune its own models, that is out of scope for this module and needs
> separate, tailored provisions.

## Model language

> Draft language for attorney review. Not legal advice. Reconcile
> with `clause-playbook.md` and the state carve-out for the employee's state.

**Nondisclosure of Confidential Information:**

> "Without limiting the foregoing, I will not enter, upload, paste, transmit, disclose, or
otherwise make available any Confidential Information to any AI Tool unless the AI Tool and the
applicable use case have been approved by Company in accordance with Company's Artificial
Intelligence Use Policy, as amended from time to time. I will comply with Company's Artificial
Intelligence Use Policy, including any list of approved AI Tools, approved use cases, access
requirements, confidentiality restrictions, and reporting obligations."

**AI definitions** — add to the agreement's definitions:

- **AI Tools** means any artificial-intelligence or machine-learning system, whether
  generative (such as large language models or image, audio, or code generators) or
  algorithmic (such as predictive, classification, ranking, or decision-support
  systems), including models, APIs, agents, applications, and services, whether
  provided by a third party or deployed internally by the Company.
- **AI-Assisted Work Product** means any work product, invention, discovery, idea, process,
formula, software, code, model, design, documentation, content, analysis, output, material, or
other work that is created, developed, authored, modified, enhanced, summarized, analyzed, or
generated, in whole or in part, using or with the assistance of any AI Tool.

**AI-related Confidential Information:**

> "Confidential Information also means non-public information relating to Company's use,
development, evaluation, procurement, integration, or operation of AI Tools, including Company
prompts, prompt libraries, system prompts, system instructions, agent configurations, model
selection criteria, confidential information received from AI vendors, internal benchmarks,
evaluations, test results, red-team results, known capabilities and limitations of AI Tools as used
by Company, AI workflows, and AI-related product, engineering, security, and operational practices."

**Inventions include AI-Assisted Work Product:**

> "Company Inventions include AI-Assisted Work Product, prompts, prompt libraries, system prompts,
system instructions, agent configurations, AI workflows, model/tool evaluation materials, and other
AI-related materials created, developed, authored, modified, or reduced to practice within the
scope of my employment or using Company equipment, supplies, facilities, or Confidential
Information."
